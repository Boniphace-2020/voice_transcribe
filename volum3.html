About Us

We are passionate developers from the University of Dodoma (UDOM), dedicated to leveraging emerging technologies to transform learning experiences. Our mission is to enhance education through voice-driven applications that make learning more interactive, inclusive, and accessible to all — especially in regions where traditional learning tools may be limited. Our team specializes in building intelligent systems powered by speech recognition, natural language processing (NLP), and multi-language support, enabling learners to practice, interact, and receive feedback in their native language or language of study. This project is a product of both academic research and real-world needs, aiming to support students, educators, and institutions in adopting AI-powered educational tools. We believe voice technology can break barriers — making learning faster, more natural, and more human.

🛠️ Our Services

Speech-to-Text Transcription Effortlessly convert your spoken words into accurate, readable text using advanced real-time speech recognition. Ideal for taking verbal notes, documenting ideas, or capturing spoken responses during practice. Language Practice in Multiple Languages Practice speaking in a wide range of languages including English, Swahili, Mandarin, French, and Spanish. The system is designed to help learners improve pronunciation, fluency, and confidence in real-time. Downloadable Voice Transcripts Easily download your full speech transcripts in text format for offline review or sharing. Great for study records, feedback logs, or storing practice history. AI Voice Tutor Integration Learn with the help of an intelligent virtual assistant that provides instant feedback, guidance, and encouragement. Our AI tutor helps simulate real conversations and supports personalized learning paths. Visual Volume Meter A responsive visual indicator that displays your speaking volume in real-time, helping you manage voice clarity and projection during practice. Secure User Login & Access Control Ensures that only authorized users can access the app’s full features. Your data and recordings remain protected with simple login verification. Offline-Friendly Recording Access Recordings and transcripts can be downloaded for use in areas with limited internet access, enabling inclusive learning even in remote environments. Educational Support Use-Case Ready Perfect for use in schools, universities, and training centers that aim to incorporate interactive and voice-assisted technologies into their curriculum.

🏫 Our Background

We are a team of passionate innovators and software engineers from the College of Informatics and Virtual Education (CIVE) at University of Dodoma (UDOM). Our mission is to use technology to enhance accessibility, learning, and voice-based educational tools across Tanzania and beyond.

We began this initiative as a student-led research project, now evolving into an impactful tool that supports language practice, transcription, and AI tutoring using Swahili, English, Chinese, French, and more.
