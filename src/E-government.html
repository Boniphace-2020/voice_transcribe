Telecommunication Infrastructure Index (TII)

Investing in telecom networks ensures wider coverage and bandwidth for effective e-government services.

Online Service Index (OSI)

Expanding user-friendly online services improves accessibility, including online payments and transactions.

Human Capital Index (HCI)

Education and training enhance digital literacy among government employees and citizens.

Government-to-Government (G2G)

The e-Government Authority (e-GA) ensures compliance and cybersecurity in government data sharing.

Government-to-Citizen (G2C)

Citizens access various government services via mobile platforms.

Government-to-Employee (G2E)

Training programs equip government employees with essential e-governance skills.

Government-to-Business (G2B)

Online registration simplifies business setup and management.

Success & Challenges

Success: National e-Procurement System (NeST)

NeST has streamlined procurement, saved costs, and improved efficiency.

Challenges

Infrastructure deficiencies, technical issues, cybersecurity threats, and limited accessibility remain challenges.
