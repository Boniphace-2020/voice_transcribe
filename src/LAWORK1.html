About

Here you can learn more about us The people, and skills – all in short supply in the public sector. For many public organizations, a centralized approach may not be possible because of financial constraints; because staff are too busy on other things; or because no-one has the confidence or capa- bilities to undertake the necessary planning and coordination tasks. The people, and skills – all in short supply in the public sector. For many public organizations, a centralized approach may not be possible because of financial constraints; because staff are too busy on other things; or Sometimes, skills and awareness may be present within the organization, but not in the right place. Centralized approaches require involve- ment of senior staff, yet it is they who are often the last in the public sector to pick up IT or systems skills. Partly related to this is the perception of costs and benefits. The costs of decentralized approaches – of wasted efforts, of organiza- tional inflexibility, of staff time diverted from other activities – are frequently hidden. The benefits, such as managers being able to do what they like, are fairly obvious. For central- ized approaches, by contrast, the equation is often the other way round. Services

Here we dldescribe the services we offer to our customers Internet of Things (IoT) The Internet of Things (IoT) describes a technology whereby physical objects are connected to the Internet and are able to communicate with, and identify themselves to, other devices These devices (sensors, computing devices,smart devices, networking and storage equipment), technologies and the communication between these devices can benefit e- government by providing enough quality data to generate the information required to make the right decisions at the right time. The IoT is important because a physical object that is able to communicate digitally is able to relate not only to a single entity, but also becomes connected to surrounding objects and data infrastructures. This allows for a situation in which many physical objects are able to act in unison, by means of ambient intelligence.
